SOFTWARE ARCHITECTS, INC. OPENS SAN DIEGO OFFICE

San Diego -- Saturday, July 01, 2000 --

Software Architects, Inc., a leading e-commerce solutions provider for Fortune 500 and dot-com companies, today announced the opening of its San Diego office.  The company is currently looking to hire top-notch IT consultants for the new office.

“Software Architects opened its first West Coast office in San Diego because of its thriving business community and large demand for superior technology support,” said Bill Tanca, Software Architects’ San Diego branch manager.  “The new office will provide the best technical solutions for this booming area.”

Software Architects will continue to expand its West Coast presence with plans to open two new offices per year.

Founded in 1978, Software Architects is an $80 million company with more than 800 employees and consultants in offices nationwide.  The company’s client roster includes Qwest Communications, Victoria’s Secret Catalogue, Digital Storage, BMW Financial Services, and Verio.  Software Architects is a partner-level Microsoft Solution Provider.

Software Architects is headquartered in Chicago, Ill., with offices in Atlanta, Cincinnati, Cleveland, Columbus, Dallas, Denver, Houston, Indianapolis, Minneapolis, Phoenix, Tampa and  San Diego.  For more information on Software Architects’ services or employee opportunities visit www.sark.com.
