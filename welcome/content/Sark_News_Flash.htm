Marketing and Branding

 

One of the most frequently asked questions during the Stock Option Presentations was where the company stood on improving our brand awareness and marketing plans. Or more directly, “What are you guys doing with all that Fremont money?” Well here goes: For those of you who have not yet seen it, I’m very excited to announce the unveiling of our new web site. (http://www.sark.com/). While there are a number of updates and improvements still to be made, it is clearly a big step in the entire process of improving our name recognition. Beyond that, I’m happy to report that we are well on our way to finishing the next phase of this process. Without going into too much detail this means that we have outsourced our Corporate Branding and Name Recognition requirements to BSMG Worldwide Visual Communications. This is a 4 to 6 week process whereby we will review our image, past history and future direction, as well as gathering some customer perceptions about us. They will analyze all of our graphic materials and we will receive recommendations for any potential changes. Also included will be recommendations for “positioning statements” on Software Architects. We will also receive a stationary package, which will have recommended layouts for new stationary, business cards etc.  The last deliverable will be a Graphics Standards Manual. This manual would then help us as we go forward into the next phase, which would include developing all of our new collateral material. This would include things like new sales/marketing and recruiting pieces. This is an exciting new process for the company and will ultimately result in our name and our capabilities becoming better recognized across the country.
