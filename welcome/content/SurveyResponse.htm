Intranet Survey Results

 

I know everyone is dying to know that results of the Intranet Survey so here they are.  One a scale of 1 to 5 (with 1 being the BEST and 5 being the worst) the intranet got an overall score of 2.13. 

 

The following is a complete list of the suggested new items that people would like to see on the Intranet.  I have responded to each request.  There are several items that will be placed on the Intranet Project List.  If you have any questions about any of the following items or the reason for not placing it on the list please contact me.

 

 

  1. New Timesheet system integration (i.e. Similar to e-mail) – This will be a link off of the Corporate Intranet site, we will provide a link off of the intranet as soon as the production server has been defined.

 

  1. Interoffice games ie. fantasy football league – Games are maintained on non-sark servers for several reasons, if you can’t think of any please call me and I’d be more than happy to list them.

 

  1. Sponsor information – Coming soon.  As soon as Breuer gets around to it ;-)

 

  1. Solution Services forum section for tech-related info – We have a solution services document repository in place, a forum for technology questions/discussion is a good idea.  This will be placed on the Intranet Project List.

 

  1. Access to exam material online (i.e., Transcenders, Practice Tests, etc.) – Most of the transcenders and practice exams that we have are single user licenses.  By placing them on the Intranet we would be violating our licensing agreement with those vendors.

 

  1. Secure way to download contact information in Outlook/pda format – Not sure what you are trying to do with this.  You can download the Client List and Office Directory off of the Intranet via AvantGo (contact Jim Kriz).  If you are looking for something different please contact me to discuss.

 

  1. More access to files that are on the "Public" drive – What do you want.  After reviewing the files that are located in the public drives, I can’t think of too many things that you would need access to?  If there is something specific, we can post it on the intranet.  Please let me know and I would be more than happy to discuss this.

 

  1. Chat – I don’t think I need to explain why we don’t have this on the intranet.

 

  1. Get rid of the dhtml menu on the left - it's just plain annoying – I am more than happy to change this provided someone gives me a better idea.  A better idea is not the static menu that we used to have.  It must be dynamic and provide the ability to only display the main menu items on the initial page load.  Jim Kriz has presented an alternative solution and we are looking into it.

  1. No DHTML Menus – See # 9
