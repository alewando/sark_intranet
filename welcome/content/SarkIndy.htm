SOFTWARE ARCHITECTS, INC. OPENS INDIANAPOLIS OFFICE

Indianapolis -- Saturday, July 01, 2000 --

Software Architects, Inc., a leading e-commerce solutions provider for Fortune 500 and dot-com companies, today announced the opening of its Indianapolis office.  The company is currently looking to hire top-notch IT consultants for this new location.

“Indianapolis is a thriving business community with a great demand for superior technology support,” said Dave Kathan, Software Architects’ Indianapolis branch manager. 

As a result of the city’s tremendous business growth, the Indianapolis office will be a significant part of Software Architects’ Midwest presence.

Founded in 1978, Software Architects is an $80 million company with more than 800 employees and consultants in offices nationwide.  The company’s client roster includes Qwest Communications, Victoria’s Secret Catalogue, Digital Storage, BMW Financial Services, and Verio.  Software Architects is a partner-level Microsoft Solution Provider.

Software Architects is headquartered in Chicago, Ill., with offices in Atlanta, Cincinnati, Cleveland, Columbus, Dallas, Denver, Houston, Minneapolis, Indianapolis, Phoenix, Tampa, and San Diego.   For more information on Software Architects’ services or employment opportunities visit www.sark.com.
